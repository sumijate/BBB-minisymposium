Transportation

By plane

The closest international airports are in Budapest, Vienna or Belgrade.

Transport from Budapest is possible by train: https://jegy.mav.hu

Shuttle transport via car is available to order from the airport: https://zoldtranszfer.hu/eng.html

Please contact us directly if you need help with organizing the transportation.

By bus

There are buses available between Budapest and Szeged, from the „Népliget” central bus station.

By car

You can park directly at the Biological Research Centre. Please submit your plate number beforehand. Alternatively parking spots can be found in the streets around the building.
